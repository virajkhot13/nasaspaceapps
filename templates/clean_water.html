    What Is Clean Water

    Definition

    Clean water refers to water that is safe for drinking and suitable for various other purposes without the risk of contamination. It is essential for human health and the environment.

    Importance

    Clean water is crucial for hydration, sanitation, agriculture, and sustaining aquatic ecosystems. It helps prevent waterborne diseases and supports life on Earth.

    Sources

    Clean water can be sourced from natural springs, rivers, lakes, and treated municipal water supplies. Proper treatment and filtration are essential to ensure safety.
