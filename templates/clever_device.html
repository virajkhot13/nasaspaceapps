Real-time Water Cleanliness Calculator

Our Clever Device provides a real-time water cleanliness calculator that continuously monitors and analyzes water quality. You can instantly access accurate information about the cleanliness of the water source.

Fast and Reliable

With cutting-edge technology, our device delivers fast and reliable results. You can rely on it for quick assessments of water quality, ensuring you make informed decisions.

Connectivity with Clever Water App

Seamlessly connect our Clever Device with the Clever Water App. Monitor water quality data remotely, receive alerts, and contribute to the collective effort to ensure clean and safe water sources.

Our Technology

It is a Device which measures following parameters like pH,Turbidity,Viruses,Temperature and odour of water.In this data is collectedby a microcontroller. It allows us to access real-time water quality data through a dashboard/app.
