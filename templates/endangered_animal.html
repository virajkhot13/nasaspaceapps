    Endangered Aquatic Animals

    Dolphin

    The dolphin is a highly intelligent marine mammal known for its playful behavior and social interactions. However, many dolphin species are facing threats due to habitat loss, pollution, and fishing activities.

    Sea Turtle

    Sea turtles are ancient reptiles that have inhabited the oceans for millions of years. Unfortunately, they are now endangered due to factors such as poaching, habitat destruction, and accidental capture in fishing gear.

    Whale Shark

    The whale shark is the largest fish in the world, but it is also listed as a vulnerable species. Threats to whale sharks include hunting, ship strikes, and habitat degradation.
